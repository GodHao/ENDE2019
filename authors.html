Guidelines for Oral presentation


Time limit for each oral presentation is based on the type of presentation:


Regular oral presentations: 12 minutes for presentation, 3 minutes for discussion, total 15 minutes.


Invited Talks: 25 minutes for presentation, 5 minutes for discussion, total 30 minutes.


Plenary Talk: 50 minutes for presentation, and 10 minutes for discussion, total 60 minutes.


All speakers are requested to report their attendance to the session chairpersons 10 minutes before the start of the session and load their presentations (MS POWERPOINT compatible or PDF format) onto the available PC (Windows operating system).


Guidelines for Poster presentation


Poster sessions will be held in the Exhibit Hall of the conference venue. Please use a standard poster size such as 48 inches (H) X 36 inches (W) or 1200 mm (H) X 900mm (W).


During poster sessions, no other event will be programmed. Students who are participating in the “Student Poster Competition” are encouraged to present a 3-minute technical talk with Q&A. This talk will be recorded.
