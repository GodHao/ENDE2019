The 24th International Workshop on Electromagnetic Nondestructive Evaluation

(ENDE2019)

Longemont Hotels in Chengdu, Sichuan, China

September 11th - September 14th

The 24th International Workshop on Electromagnetic Nondestructive Evaluation (ENDE2019) will be held on September 11-14, 2019, at the Longemont Hotels in Chengdu, Sichuan, China.

This year's technical sessions, student posters competition, industry exhibition, tour, and other special events will be coordinated by University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, Sichuan University and Southwest Jiaotong University. The subjects of the 24th workshop will focus on the theoretical and applied research on Electromagnetic Non-destructive Evaluation methods and provide a wonderful opportunity to exchange ideas and update on the latest developments.

We welcome you to join us for an exciting program in “the Land of Abunddance” in 2019!

Call for Paper: [1st CFP in PDF (30/10/2018); 2nd CFP in PDF]

Authors are invited to submit short papers up to two pages relevant to the workshop topics. Papers should present original work not submitted or published elsewhere. Short papers are to be reviewed by our Scientific Committee. The accepted ones will be included in the workshop proceeding. Authors of accepted short papers are encouraged to submit full papers between 4-6 pages at the workshop, which will be peer reviewed for inclusion in a volume of the ISTP (EI) indexed book series “Studies on Applied Electromagnetic and Mechanics”, published by IOS Press. Outstanding papers will be recommended to SCI indexed journal such as Sensors.

List of Topics:

Advanced ENDE sensors

Analytical and numerical modeling of ENDE

Integrated ENDT&E systems

Inverse problems, imaging and signal processing

QNDE and AI for ENDE

Material characterization (damages and micro-defects)

Monitoring and diagnosis of mechanical structures

New developments in ENDE

Innovative industrial applications of ENDE
