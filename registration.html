Registration Fees


1.Regular registration: regular registration fee is per paper per person, which includes the book of abstracts, IOS Press Special Issue book, welcome reception, lunches, banquet, coffee breaks and participation in all related workshop social events.
2.Student registration: discounted registration fee is per paper per student, which includes all privileges of regular registration plus participation of student special events and poster competition.
3. Accompanying Person: registration fee for accompanying person includes all lunches, banquet and coffee breaks. Each author can only register for one accompanying person.
P.S.: For the convenience of the participants, we also provide poster printing services. The price is 15 dollars per poster.
