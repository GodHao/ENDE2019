Registration Fees


1.Regular registration: regular registration fee is per paper per person, which includes the book of abstracts, IOS Press Special Issue book, welcome reception, lunches, banquet, coffee breaks and participation in all related workshop social events. A ticket worth 58 yuan for the panda base will be applied to attendees who paid regular registration fee and will have more than one paper accepted for publication in the proceedings and special issue book.
2.Student registration: discounted registration fee is per paper per student, which includes all privileges of regular registration plus participation of student special events and poster competition and A ticket worth 58 yuan for the panda base.
3.Exhibit registration: registration fee is per exhibitor per booth, which includes one (1) complimentary regular registration and privileges.
4.Student volunteer registration: ENDE2018 offer a limited number of complimentary volunteer registration, which needs to be approved by the organizing committee after online registration.Proof of student status might be required.
